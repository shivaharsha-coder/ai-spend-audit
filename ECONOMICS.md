# Economics

## Problem
Many users subscribe to multiple AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, etc., but they do not track their total monthly spending.  
This leads to unnecessary subscriptions and wasted money.

## Why This Matters
AI tools are becoming essential for students and professionals.  
Without proper tracking, users may overspend without realizing it.

## Target Users
- Students using AI for learning
- Freelancers using AI for productivity
- Small teams sharing subscriptions

## Solution
AI Spend Audit analyzes user input and categorizes spending:
- High → needs optimization
- Moderate → can improve usage
- Optimized → no action needed

It also suggests possible savings.

## Value Proposition
- Helps users save money
- Gives clarity on tool usage
- Fast and simple analysis

## Pricing (Future Scope)
- Free: Basic audit
- Pro: Detailed insights + history
- Team: Multi-user tracking

## Revenue Model
Subscription-based SaaS product (future scope).
