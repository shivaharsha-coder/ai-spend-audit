# Pricing Data

## Overview
The AI Spend Audit product follows a simple SaaS pricing model designed for different types of users.

---

## Plans

### 1. Free Plan
- Basic audit functionality
- Limited usage per day
- No history tracking

Best for:
- Students
- First-time users

---

### 2. Pro Plan (Future)
- Advanced spending insights
- Detailed savings suggestions
- Audit history tracking
- Better recommendations

Best for:
- Freelancers
- Individual professionals

---

### 3. Team Plan (Future)
- Multi-user access
- Shared dashboards
- Team-level spending insights
- Usage tracking across users

Best for:
- Startups
- Small teams

---

## Pricing Strategy

- Start with a free product to attract users
- Introduce Pro features for deeper insights
- Scale to team plans for higher revenue

---

## Why This Model Works

- Low barrier to entry (free plan)
- Users upgrade when they see value
- Teams bring higher revenue potential
